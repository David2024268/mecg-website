About MECG

Michigan Engineering Consulting Group is a pro-bono consulting group open to all majors at the University of Michigan.

MECG places an emphasis on a community of diverse students that share one thing: a passion to learn more about consulting and gain hands-on experiences in the field. Our four pillars (Professional Development, Education, Project Experience, and Community) drive our impact on real companies and members’ careers.

President’s Welcome

Hi there! Welcome to MECG’s official website! Thank you for your interest in MECG and exploring what we have to offer. Founded by a group of like-minded engineers, we are committed to delivering high-impact solutions while providing consulting opportunities for students.

When I joined MECG as a part of the first class of new members, I was looking for a space where I could further my knowledge of consulting as an engineer and build strong connections with others. As President, I strive to ensure that MECG offers just that: an organization where all students, regardless of their background or experience, feel welcomed and supported.

At MECG, the only quality that we look for in students is the eagerness to learn more about the consulting world. I believe that anyone who wishes to broaden their understanding in consulting should feel empowered to do so. By providing a full education curriculum, wide range of project experiences, and early career opportunities, MECG is here to help you grow.

I encourage you to step out of your comfort zone and seek the professional growth and community that MECG has to offer. Please don’t hesitate to contact us at mecg-board@umich.edu or follow @mecgmichigan on Instagram for more updates.

Best,
Jules Hwang
President of MECG, 2025
