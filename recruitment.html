Recruitment

MECG is recruiting! Each semester, we welcome applicants from all backgrounds and disciplines. No prior consulting experience is required—just a curiosity to learn and a willingness to contribute.

All University of Michigan students are eligible to apply regardless of major, year, or experience level!

We’re looking for driven individuals with a growth mindset who are excited to learn about consulting and contribute to our community.

Roughly 5–6 hours per week during project seasons, including weekly meetings, project work, and workshops.

Follow us on Instagram (@mecgmichigan) and check our website each semester for the application and info session dates!
